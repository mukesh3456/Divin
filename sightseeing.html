Divine Boutique hotel is positioned on a hillside and located just 1 km from the very popular On Wheelz Amusement Park on the Ruighar – Mahu Dam Road, near Ganesh Mandir, Ganeshpeth. You can also explore the beautiful scenic views of Parsi Point and Bhilar Waterfall just 1.5 km away. Besides, most of the famous tourist places in Panchgani are also easily accessible from the hotel. These include Table Land- 3 km away, Sidney Point– 5 km, Velocity Entertainmentz- 5.5 km, and the Mapro Garden which is in a 7 km radius. Sightseeing in Panchgani

Table Point

It is only a 13-minute drive from Hotel Divine Boutique to this enormous, flat expanse of laterite rock, which is the second-longest alpine plateau in Asia. From here, one can see some large caves, including the "Devil's Kitchen." Numerous Indian movies have taken place in Panchgani's well-known "table-land.

Sydney Point

Sydney Point is a beautiful vantage point atop the Krishna Valley. Divine Boutique Hotel is around 13 minutes away. The sparkling water cascading over the dam and the lush, verdant valleys are sights not to be missed.

Parsi Point

Parsi Point, which is about 5 minutes away from Divine Boutique, has a view of the Krishna Valley and the Dhom Dam's lovely waters. This is the place to go if you want to take a moment to relax and just breathe. The soul-soothing air that revives any weary traveler's senses is the nicest part about it.

On Wheel amusement park

Thrilling experience at the amusement park, which offers rides for all ages, as well as a monorail, bungee jumping, and cafes, and is only 2 minutes away from our hotel. In Panchgani, you may have a lot of fun with your family and friends.

Velocity Entertainment

It is a theme park that is conveniently located for visitors of all ages and is only 11 minutes away from Divine Boutique. You can visit this pristine location with your loved ones to make some memories, whether you're a youngster, an adult, or an elderly person.

Bhilar Waterfall

The only time Bhilar Waterfall receives water is during the monsoon and winter seasons in Panchgani, Maharashtra. The waterfalls are a visual treat and the epitome of peace, calm, and tranquillity, yet they are only six minutes away from Divine Boutique.

How to Reach: Divine Boutique Hotel

The added benefit of choosing Divine Boutique Hotel is the NH48 National Highway, which is just 25 km from its vicinity. By Road, Pune to Resort is merely 100 km and Mumbai to Resort is 250 km. The nearest Bus Stop is Sanjeevan Naka just 1km away, and the nearest Railway Station is Satara, just 45 km, while the Pune railway station is around 125 km away.
